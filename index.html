Frequently Asked Questions

Is DOWNitUP a free alternative to IDM?

Yes. DOWNitUP offers the same multi-connection download acceleration as Internet Download Manager (IDM), but it's completely free with no trial period or license fees. Plus, it works on macOS, Linux, and Android too — not just Windows.

How does DOWNitUP compare to ADM (Advanced Download Manager)?

DOWNitUP provides the same fast HTTP downloads as ADM with the added benefit of built-in BitTorrent support and availability on desktop platforms (Windows, macOS, Linux), not just Android.

Can I use DOWNitUP instead of qBittorrent?

DOWNitUP supports magnet links and torrent downloads just like qBittorrent, while also offering fast multi-connection HTTP downloads. It's a unified solution for all your downloading needs.

Is DOWNitUP safe and private?

Absolutely. DOWNitUP collects zero personal data, includes no trackers or analytics, and stores all download information locally on your device. Your privacy is fully respected.
