macOS Installation Guide

1

Open the installer

Locate the downloaded DOWNitUP-1.0.0.dmg file in your Downloads folder and double-click to open it.

2

Drag to Applications

Drag the DOWNitUP icon into the Applications shortcut folder in the window that appears.

3

Attempt to run the app

Open your Applications directory and double-click DOWNitUP. A system warning dialog will appear stating that the app is from an unidentified developer. Click OK or close the dialog.

4

Navigate to Settings

Open your Mac's System Settings (or System Preferences) from the Apple Menu in the top left corner and go to Privacy & Security.

5

Allow Untrusted App

Scroll down to the "Security" section. You will see a note saying "DOWNitUP was blocked from use because it is not from an identified developer". Click Open Anyway.

6

Confirm launch

Authenticate using your administrator password or Touch ID, then click Open on the final dialog to run the app. Subsequent launches will open normally without warnings.
